What is the process for getting a root canal at Advanced Emerald Dentistry?
Your root canal treatment starts with a thorough exam and digital X-rays so we can assess the tooth and plan your procedure. After numbing the area, our endodontist gently removes the infected pulp, cleans and shapes the root canals, and fills them with a biocompatible material. In most cases, the tooth is then sealed with a temporary filling, and we’ll schedule a follow-up to place a permanent restoration, such as a crown, to protect your tooth.
